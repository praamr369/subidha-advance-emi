# First Day Admin Checklist

## Before opening collections

- Confirm the active Business Profile is correct.
- Confirm the active head office or branch mapping is correct.
- Confirm cash, bank, and UPI finance accounts are active.
- Confirm at least one active cash desk is mapped to the correct branch.
- Confirm at least one staff assignment can collect payments.
- Confirm at least one staff assignment can verify payments if your workflow requires dual control.
- Confirm chart accounts exist for cash, bank, and revenue classification.
- Confirm the checklist page shows 100% completion and ready for go-live.

## Operational notes

- Finance accounts are setup masters only in this pass. Existing payment posting logic remains unchanged.
- Chart accounts are classification masters only in this pass. They are not wired into payment posting.
- Retire setup records by deactivating them. Do not delete referenced masters.
