# Cashier / Counter Opening and Closing Checklist

## Opening

1. Confirm cashier user is assigned to the intended active counter.
2. Confirm the counter belongs to the intended branch.
3. Confirm the counter finance account is the intended cash, bank, or UPI book.
4. Confirm the previous day was closed or explicitly handed over.
5. Perform one read-only payment search before taking the first collection.

## During the day

- Use only the assigned counter for collections.
- Do not collect money under another cashier login.
- Do not reuse another branch counter for convenience.
- Record any failed collection attempts or branch-assignment issues immediately.

## Closing

1. Reconcile the counter’s cash, bank, and UPI totals against the payment register.
2. Review duplicate-reference warnings or reversals.
3. Confirm any pending mismatch is escalated before logout.
4. Hand over physical cash and note the handoff outside the app if required by ops policy.
5. Log out from the cashier session.
