# Customer Profile Operations

## Goal

Give admins one operational profile for customer history while keeping direct-sale and subscription financial controls separate.

## Profile coverage

`Admin > Customers > Customer Detail` now surfaces:

- customer identity and KYC state
- subscription portfolio
- direct-sale portfolio and outstanding
- payment history
- receipts, invoices, and documents
- lead/quotation/estimate history
- ledger summary and partner linkage context

## Daily usage

1. Verify customer identity, contact details, and KYC posture.
2. Review direct-sale outstanding and open direct-sale collection actions.
3. Review subscription contract/payment posture separately.
4. Inspect lead/quotation history for context before next sale or follow-up.
5. Open invoices/receipts/documents from the same page for audit traceability.

## Operational controls

- Direct-sale receivables stay separate from subscription EMI posting logic.
- Lead history visibility does not alter financial records.
- Invoice, receipt, and payment links remain route-safe drill-downs to source systems.
