# Vendor & Supplier Management

- Vendor module extends existing `accounting.Vendor` as the single vendor master.
- Admin can manage vendors, categories, service areas, products, quote requests, sourcing suggestions, ledger, outstanding, and account-link controls.
- Vendor portal is role-scoped (`VENDOR`) and exposes only linked-vendor data.
- Sourcing suggestions are read-only recommendations and do not auto-create purchase orders.
