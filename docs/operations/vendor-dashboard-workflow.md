# Vendor Dashboard Workflow

## Purpose

Provide a production vendor portal with procurement-specific operational visibility.

## Dashboard Scope

- Pending quote requests
- Accepted quotes
- Purchase orders
- Purchase returns
- Vendor ledger and outstanding payable
- Vendor products/documents
- Vendor-safe notifications

## Quick Actions

- Submit Quote
- View Purchase Orders
- View Ledger
- View Outstanding
- Update Products
- Open Notification Center

## Guardrails

- No cross-vendor visibility
- No customer EMI details
- No partner commission data
- No admin-only margins/reconciliation controls
