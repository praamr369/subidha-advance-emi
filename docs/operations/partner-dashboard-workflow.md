# Partner Dashboard Workflow

## Purpose

Provide partner-scoped visibility for referred customers, subscriptions, commissions, and payouts.

## Dashboard Scope

- Partner summary (customers, subscriptions, collection posture)
- Commission earned and pending commission
- Payout posture and statement access
- Lead/subscription request follow-up
- Partner-safe notifications

## Quick Actions

- Submit Lead / Customer Request
- Open My Customers
- Open Commissions
- Open Payouts
- Open Statements

## Guardrails

- No cross-partner customer leakage
- No admin-only reconciliation/accounting controls
- No vendor internals
