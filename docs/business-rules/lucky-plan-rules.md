# Lucky Plan Rules

## Core rules
- Product base price is treated as total contract price
- Default EMI is total contract price divided by tenure months
- A subscription belongs to a customer, product, batch, and lucky ID
- Winner receives waiver of future EMI only
- Past paid EMIs are never refunded through the winner flow unless explicitly handled by a separate audited business rule
- All payment, waiver, and status changes must be auditable

## Operational rule
The system must remain simple enough for daily shop use.
